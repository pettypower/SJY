# Html表格

> 撰稿人：邹强  
> 基本简介：Html表格笔记  
> 作成时间：2019-7-27  
> 修改时间：2019-7-27

1. `<table>` 表格
2. `<th>` 标题栏
3. `<tr>` 表格的行
4. `<td>` 表格的列
5. colspan 属性
6. rowspan 属性

```html
    <table border = "1"  width = "400" height = "150">

        <thead>
            <tr>
            <td colspan = "4">This is the head of the table</td>
            </tr>
        </thead>

        <tfoot>
            <tr>
            <td colspan = "4">This is the foot of the table</td>
            </tr>
        </tfoot>
        <tbody>
        <caption>这是标题</caption>
            <tr>
                <th>Column 1</th>
                <th>Column 2</th>
                <th>Column 3</th>
            </tr>
            <tr>
                <td rowspan = "2">Row 1 Cell 1</td>
                <td>Row 1 Cell 2</td>
                <td>Row 1 Cell 3</td>
            </tr>
            <tr>
                <td>Row 2 Cell 2</td>
                <td>Row 2 Cell 3</td>
            </tr>
            <tr>
                <td colspan = "3">Row 3 Cell 1</td>
            </tr>
        </tbody>
    </table>
```
